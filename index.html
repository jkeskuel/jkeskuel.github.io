Independent SQL Server consultant based in Slovenia. I help teams keep their data layer out of the way of everything else.

About

I've been working with SQL Server for the better part of two decades — government, finance, utilities, the usual suspects. Most of what I do is unglamorous: tune the slow query, untangle the HA setup, plan the migration so nobody calls anyone at 3 a.m.

I work independently, by the hour or by the project. No sales pitch, no decks, no "synergies". If your databases are misbehaving or you just want a second pair of eyes, get in touch.

What I do

  • Performance tuning. Queries, indexes, schemas, the occasional bad ORM decision.
  • High availability & DR. Always On, log shipping, failover clusters — designed so they actually work when you need them.
  • Migrations. Version upgrades, consolidations, on-prem to cloud. Boring is the goal.
  • Cost & licensing review. You're probably overpaying somewhere. Let's find out where.
  • Dev support. Helping app teams write data layers that age well.
  • DevOps for databases. Schema in source control, pipelines, repeatable deployments.
  • Mentoring. One-on-one or small team. I'd rather teach you than sell you a retainer.
